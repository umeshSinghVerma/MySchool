Specifications

  • Multi-Device Compatibility:

    Upon signup, our system conducts camera calibration to recognize screen boundaries. Whether you're using a desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphone, our system accurately detects attentiveness through eye movement, ensuring seamless functionality across all devices.

  • Revision Helper:

    During the academic year, our system evaluates student attentiveness, particularly during exam periods. By identifying students who may be struggling based on their engagement levels in lectures, we tailor specific courses to address their weaknesses. This personalized approach ensures improved performance and better exam outcomes, providing a customized learning experience for each student.

  • Seamless Integration:

    Our system seamlessly integrates with various teaching platforms such as Webex, Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, etc. Teachers have the flexibility to conduct live video sessions or share pre-recorded videos and textual references. In all scenarios, the system diligently monitors student attention, recording valuable data for personalized student engagement strategies.

  • Feedback Mechanism :

    Students receive alerts if their face is not detected in the camera frame, prompting them to adjust their position for better engagement. Additionally, if a significant portion of the class shows low attentiveness, feedback is provided to the teacher, suggesting the incorporation of interactive elements to enhance student participation.

  • Interactive Quizzes:

    Upon completion of tasks, students will engage in quizzes that include questions from points where their attentiveness was low. If a student answers over 50% of these questions correctly, it's deemed satisfactory. However, if performance falls below, the system advises revisiting the corresponding lecture segments for better understanding.

  • Teacher Monitoring:

    The platform enables the analysis of student attentiveness trends to evaluate the effectiveness of teaching methods. By identifying instances where a considerable number of students exhibit consistently low attentiveness, the system facilitates informed decisions regarding teacher evaluation or support interventions. This feature aims to ensure an optimal learning environment by fostering continuous improvement in teaching practices.
